Understanding PCOS

PCOS is a hormonal disorder marked by excess androgen, causing irregular periods, hair growth, acne, and weight gain. It can lead to fertility issues and increase the risk of diabetes and heart disease. Homeopathy treats PCOS by addressing the root cause, balancing hormones, and managing symptoms safely. Dr Batra’s® homeopathic treatments restore hormonal balance naturally

Symptoms of PCOS

Irregular or missed periods

Acne, oily skin, & unwanted hair growth

Unexplained weight gain or difficulty losing weight

Hair thinning or infertility

Causes of PCOS

Genetic factors

Insulin resistance leads to hormonal imbalance

Lifestyle factors such as poor diet & stress
